## LocusPin — Local Discovery, Reimagined
<img url="https://github.com/Psted/LocusPin/blob/main/eng_feature-graphic.png"></img>

LocusPin helps you discover what’s happening right around you. It shows nearby businesses, events, and community posts on an interactive map and in an easy-to-scan feed, so you can quickly see local recommendations, real‑time updates, and conversations. Notify users within range whenever they reach an area that is pinned dangerous or insecure so that they are aware! 

Key things you can do
- See nearby posts and businesses on a map with clustered pins for clarity.  
- Browse a chronological feed of local updates and highlights.  
- Create posts with text and media tied to a real location.  
- Comment and engage with nearby conversations.  
- Get notified about new posts, replies, and relevant local updates.  
- Manage a business presence to share updates and correct location details.  
- Report inappropriate content and rely on moderation tools to keep the community safe.  
- Control preferences like notifications and basic profile info.  

Experience
- Focused on hyperlocal discovery and community connection.  
- Clean, modern interface designed for quick browsing and real‑time engagement.  
- Built to help residents, visitors, and local businesses connect around places and moments.
